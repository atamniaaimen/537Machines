Clear Space

Always maintain minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "5" character. This ensures legibility and presence in all contexts.

✓ Do

  • Use the logo on approved background colors
  • Maintain the split-block structure at all times
  • Use the white-green variant on green backgrounds
  • Use the monochrome version for single-color contexts
  • Keep minimum size of 120px wide for digital

✕ Don't

  • Rotate, skew, or distort the logo
  • Change the logo colors or add gradients
  • Add drop shadows, outlines, or effects
  • Rearrange or resize the split-block panels
  • Place on busy backgrounds or photographs
